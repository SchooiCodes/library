Gaming Piracy Guide

Download games — repacks, DLC unlockers, emulators, and ROMs.

Table of Contents (3 sections)

  • Game Download Sites

Game Download Sites

FitGirl Repacks: most popular — highly compressed installers (50-80% smaller), reliable, pre-cracked. DODI Repacks: similar to FitGirl, alternative selection. SteamRIP: Steam games unpacked from Steam files (no crack needed for some). GOG-games: DRM-free GOG releases. Online-Fix: cracked multiplayer for popular games. ElAmigos: clean, uncompressed releases. CS.RIN.RU: forum with game releases, updates, DLC unlocks.

Emulators & ROMs

Emulators: RetroArch (all systems), Ryujinx/Yuzu (Switch), RPCS3 (PS3), Xenia (Xbox 360), PCSX2 (PS2), DuckStation (PS1), Citra (3DS — development stopped but works), mGBA (GBA), PPSSPP (PSP). ROMs: Vimm's Lair (safe, curated), CDRomance (retro), Edge Emulation (direct play in browser), Nyaa (for Switch games), Internet Archive (abandonware, ROM sets). BIOS files are legally required for PS1/PS2 emulation.

DLC Unlockers & Tools

Steam DLC unlockers: CreamAPI (add DLC entries to Steam emulator), ScreamAPI (for Epic Games), Steam-Account-Manager (manage alt accounts). Goldberg Emulator: bypass Steam for LAN/online play with friends (supports Steamworks). Koalageddon: Universal DLC unlocker for Steam, Epic, Origin. Tools: Steamless (unpack SteamStub DRM), rePACK by ElAmigos. FitGirl repacks include all DLC and updates pre-installed.
