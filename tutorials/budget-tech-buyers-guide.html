Budget Tech Buyer's Guide 2026

Best tech under $200 — monitors, headphones, mice, keyboards, SSDs, and more.

Monitors under $200

$100-150: Dell S2421NX (24" IPS 1080p 75Hz). ASUS VA24EHE (24" IPS). AOC 24G2SP (24" IPS 165Hz gaming).

$150-200: HP X27q (27" IPS 1440p 165Hz — incredible value). LG 27UP600 (27" 4K IPS).

Headphones & IEMs under $200

Wireless ANC ($50-100): Anker Soundcore Space Q45 (excellent ANC). Sony WH-CH720N. 1More SonoFlow.

Wired over-ear ($100-200): AKG K371 (neutral). Beyerdynamic DT 770 Pro (80 ohm). Philips Fidelio X2HR (open-back).

IEMs ($20-200): Truthear Hexa ($80). 7Hz Salnotes Zero ($20). Moondrop Blessing 2 ($200).

Mice & Keyboards under $200

Gaming mice ($20-60): Logitech G305 (wireless). Razer DeathAdder Essential. Pulsefire Haste.

Productivity mice ($40-80): Logitech M750. MX Master 3S (used).

Keyboards under $100: Keychron V1 (QMK, hotswap). Redragon K552.

Keyboards $100-200: Keychron Q1 (aluminum). NuPhy Air75 v2. Glorious GMMK Pro.

SSDs & Storage under $200

NVMe 1TB ($60-100): Teamgroup MP44L (PCIe 4.0). WD Black SN770. Solidigm P41 Plus.

NVMe 2TB ($100-160): SK Hynix P41 Platinum. Samsung 990 EVO Plus. Crucial T500.

External SSDs ($50-100): SanDisk Extreme (1050MB/s). Samsung T7 Shield.

Other Gear under $200

Wi-Fi 6 Router ($50-100): TP-Link Archer AX55. Asus RT-AX55.

Webcam ($30-80): Logitech C920s. Anker PowerConf C200 (4K).

USB-C Hub ($25-50): Anker PowerExpand+ 7-in-1. Ugreen Revodok Pro.

3D Printer ($150-200): Creality Ender 3 V3 SE. Sovol SV06.
