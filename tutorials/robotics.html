ROS 2 & Hardware

ROS 2: robot middleware. Nodes, topics, services, actions. TF: coordinate frames. URDF: robot model. RViz2: 3D visualization. Gazebo: physics sim. Micro-ROS: for MCUs. DDS: Fast DDS, Cyclone DDS. Robot types: differential drive, mecanum, manipulator. Kinematics: forward/inverse. Sensors: LiDAR, IMU, encoders, camera. Motors: DC+encoder, stepper, BLDC, servo. PID: tune for smooth control. Motor drivers: L298N, ODrive, Dynamixel.

Navigation & Vision

SLAM: GMapping, Cartographer, RTAB-Map. Nav2: global/local planner, costmap, AMCL. Behavior trees: BT.CPP. Path planning: A*, RRT. Computer vision: OpenCV, YOLO, depth cameras (RealSense, OAK-D). Visual odometry. Manipulation: MoveIt 2 (motion planning, IK). Pick and place. Autonomous driving: Autoware. Simulation: Isaac Sim. Safety: emergency stop. Single-board: Jetson, Raspberry Pi. OS: Ubuntu + ROS 2.
