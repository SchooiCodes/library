Anime & Cartoon Piracy

Stream and download anime, cartoons, and animated content — sites, torrents, and subtitles.

Table of Contents (3 sections)

  • Anime Streaming
  • Anime Torrenting
  • Subtitle Management

Anime Streaming

Current streaming sites: Aniwave (best UI, fastest updates), Zoro.to (large library), 9anime (mirrors — .to, .id, .me), AnimePahe (compressed downloads), GoGoAnime (old reliable), Marin.moe (direct downloads, highest quality). Use uBlock Origin — anime sites have aggressive popup ads. For mobile: Saikou (Android app, aggregates multiple sources), Aniyomi (Anime + manga reader, extension-based).

Anime Torrenting

Best quality comes from fansub groups on Nyaa.si (the essential anime tracker — .si domain). Quality levels: 720p x265 (small files, good quality — 200-300MB per episode), 1080p x265 (best quality/size ratio), 1080p x264 (maximum compatibility). Recommended groups: SubsPlease (720p/1080p batches, consistent quality), Erai-Raws, Judas, AnimeTime. SubsPlease batches are release-date-ordered and properly named — ideal for Sonarr/Radarr.

Subtitle Management

Subtitle sources: kitsunekko.net (large subtitle archive), AB-Subscene, Anime Subtitles (GitHub). Automation: Bazarr (auto-download subtitles for Sonarr/Radarr library), supports multiple languages. Fansub vs official: fansubs often have better translation notes, official subs are licensed and removed. Dual audio: many releases include Japanese + English audio. Tools: MKVToolNix (remux MKV audio/subtitle tracks), ffmpeg (extract/convert subs).
