Graphic Design

Graphic design — Photoshop, GIMP, vector graphics, and visual hierarchy.

Table of Contents (2 sections)

  • Raster & Vector Design
  • Composition & Principles

Raster & Vector Design

Photoshop: raster image editor — layers, masks, blending modes, adjustment layers, smart objects. Layers: organize elements — opacity, blend modes (multiply, screen, overlay, soft light). Masks: layer masks (black hides, white reveals), clipping masks (layer clipped to below). Selections: magic wand, quick selection, pen tool (paths), color range — refine edge, feather. Adjustments: curves, levels, hue/saturation, vibrance, color balance, black & white, gradient map. Filters: blur (gaussian, motion, lens), sharpen, distort, noise. GIMP: free Photoshop alternative — similar layer/mask system, extensive plugin ecosystem. Inkscape: free vector editor — SVG format. Vector vs raster: vectors (scalable, logos, illustrations), raster (pixels, photos, textures). Affinity Photo/Designer: affordable professional alternatives. Color theory: RGB (screen), CMYK (print), HEX codes, color harmony (complementary, analogous, triadic).

Composition & Principles

Design principles: hierarchy (guide eye through content), balance (symmetrical vs asymmetrical), contrast (size, color, weight), alignment (grid-based), proximity (related items group), repetition (consistency), white space (breathing room). Layout: grids (column grids, modular grids, baseline grids), golden ratio (1.618:1), rule of thirds. Typography: typeface vs font, serif vs sans-serif, kerning (space between characters), leading (line height), tracking (letter spacing), hierarchy (heading, body, caption). File formats: PSD (Photoshop), AI/EPS (Illustrator), SVG (vector web), PNG (transparency), JPEG (compressed), TIFF (print), PDF (document). Print: resolution (300 DPI), bleed (3mm extra), CMYK color mode. Brand design: logo, color palette, typography system, brand guidelines. Mockups: present designs in realistic context. Stock resources: Unsplash (photos), Freepik, Font Awesome, Google Fonts.
