Free Services Directory

Cloud Storage

Free cloud storage is excellent for backups, file sharing, and syncing across devices. Most providers offer a generous free tier with the option to upgrade. Below are the best options with their free storage limits.

Google Drive

15GB free storage shared across Drive, Gmail, and Google Photos. Integrates seamlessly with Google Workspace. Paid plans start at 100GB for $1.99/mo. No credit card required for free tier.

Limits: 15GB shared pool, 100MB file upload

CC Required: No

OneDrive

5GB free storage from Microsoft. Tightly integrated with Windows and Office Online. Camera backup on mobile. 100GB plan is $1.99/mo. Microsoft 365 includes 1TB.

Limits: 5GB, 250MB file upload

CC Required: No

Mega

20GB free end-to-end encrypted storage. Based in New Zealand, privacy-focused. Generous free tier with sync apps for all platforms. Achieve up to 35GB with bonuses.

Limits: 20GB base, transfer quota 1GB/6hr

CC Required: No

Proton Drive

End-to-end encrypted cloud storage from Proton (Switzerland). 5GB free. Zero-knowledge encryption — even Proton cannot access your files. Clean interface and mobile apps.

Limits: 5GB free

CC Required: No

pCloud

10GB free storage with Swiss-based privacy. Offers client-side encryption (pCloud Crypto) as a paid add-on. File versioning and trash retention. Lifetime plans available.

Limits: 10GB free

CC Required: No

Box

10GB free storage for personal use. Strong collaboration features, integrations with Google Workspace and Microsoft 365. 250MB file upload limit on free tier.

Limits: 10GB, 250MB file size

CC Required: No

Email Services

Privacy-respecting email providers offering free tiers with encryption, zero-access architecture, and generous storage. Essential for anyone serious about digital privacy.

Proton Mail

End-to-end encrypted email from Switzerland. 1GB storage free, 150 messages/day. Zero-access encryption. No personal information required to sign up. Paid plans start at $3.99/mo.

Limits: 1GB storage, 150 msgs/day

CC Required: No

Tutanota

End-to-end encrypted email (now Tuta). 1GB storage free, open-source, based in Germany. Encrypted calendar included. Strong privacy jurisdiction. Paid plans from 1 euro/mo.

Limits: 1GB storage, 1 calendar

CC Required: No

SimpleLogin

Email alias service (owned by Proton). 15 free aliases, unlimited replies. Hide your real email address behind unique aliases. Works with any email provider.

Limits: 15 aliases free

CC Required: No

Gmail

15GB free storage shared with Google Drive. Smart inbox, powerful filtering, and extensive integrations. Best for general use. No encryption at rest (Google has access).

Limits: 15GB shared, 50MB attachment

CC Required: No

Domain & Hosting

Free web hosting and domain services for developers, hobbyists, and small projects. These platforms offer surprisingly generous free tiers suitable for personal sites, landing pages, and static apps.

Cloudflare Pages

Free static site hosting with global CDN, SSL, and custom domains. Unlimited bandwidth and 500 builds/month. Integrates with GitHub/GitLab. Includes Cloudflare Workers for serverless functions.

Limits: 500 builds/mo, 1GB storage

CC Required: No

Netlify

Popular static hosting with continuous deployment from Git. Free tier includes 100GB bandwidth/mo, 300 build minutes/mo, SSL, and form handling. Great for Jamstack sites.

Limits: 100GB bandwidth, 300 min build

CC Required: No

Vercel

Frontend cloud by the creators of Next.js. Free tier includes 100GB bandwidth, 6000 build minutes/mo, SSL, and serverless functions. Excellent DX with automatic deployments.

Limits: 100GB bandwidth, 6000 build min

CC Required: No

GitHub Pages

Free static site hosting from GitHub repos. 1GB storage, 100GB bandwidth/mo. Automatic builds from branches. Custom domain support. Ideal for documentation and project sites.

Limits: 1GB storage, 100GB bandwidth

CC Required: No

Oracle Cloud Free Tier

Most generous cloud free tier. Get two AMD VMs (1GB RAM each) plus up to 4 ARM cores (24GB RAM) always free. 200GB block storage. Requires credit card for verification.

Limits: 2 AMD + 4 ARM OCPU, 200GB storage

CC Required: Yes (verification only)

Freenom

Free domain names in certain TLDs (.tk, .ml, .ga, .cf, .gq). Full DNS management, renewal is free. Great for testing or temporary projects. Reliability has declined recently.

Limits: 1 free domain per TLD

CC Required: No

VPN Services

Free VPN services that respect your privacy. While free tiers have limitations (server selection, speed caps), they are suitable for occasional use, bypassing geo-restrictions, or basic privacy protection.

ProtonVPN Free

No-log VPN from Proton (Switzerland). Free tier has no speed cap, no data limit, but limited to 3 countries and 1 device. Strong privacy jurisdiction. Open-source apps.

Limits: 3 countries, 1 device, medium speed

CC Required: No

Cloudflare WARP

Free VPN by Cloudflare. Encrypts all traffic and improves speed via Cloudflare's network. No logs, unlimited bandwidth, no speed cap. WARP+ uses Argo for faster routing.

Limits: Unlimited bandwidth, no speed cap

CC Required: No

Windscribe Free

Free tier: 10GB/month, 10+ countries, unlimited devices. Strong no-log policy, ad blocking included. Can earn extra 5GB by tweeting. Paid plans are also very affordable.

Limits: 10GB/mo, unlimited devices

CC Required: No

RiseupVPN

Free VPN from the Riseup collective (anarchist tech collective). No-logs, no registration needed, unlimited bandwidth. Uses Bitmask VPN client. Fewer servers but strong ethics.

Limits: Unlimited bandwidth, limited servers

CC Required: No

Development Tools

Free cloud-based development environments, CI/CD pipelines, code collaboration tools, and hosting for developers. These services enable you to build and deploy without spending a dime.

Gitpod

Cloud development environments. Free tier: 50 hours/month, 4 cores, 8GB RAM. Prebuilt environments, VS Code in browser, full Docker support. Spin up dev environments from any repo.

Limits: 50 hours/mo, 4 cores

CC Required: No

CodeSandbox

Online code editor and prototyping tool. Free tier includes public sandboxes, 1 private sandbox, basic VS Code extensions. Great for quick prototypes and sharing code snippets.

Limits: 1 private sandbox, public only

CC Required: No

GitHub Actions

CI/CD integrated with GitHub. Free tier: 2000 minutes/month, 500MB storage. Build, test, and deploy from your repos. Huge marketplace of pre-built actions.

Limits: 2000 min/mo, 500MB storage

CC Required: No

Supabase Free

Open-source Firebase alternative. Free tier: 500MB database, 1GB bandwidth, 50,000 monthly active users, 2GB storage. Includes PostgreSQL, auth, storage, and real-time subscriptions.

Limits: 500MB DB, 50K MAU, 2GB storage

CC Required: No

Glitch

Collaborative Node.js coding in the browser. Free tier: public projects, 4000 requests/hr, 512MB RAM. Great for prototyping Node.js apps, Discord bots, and web services.

Limits: 4000 req/hr, 512MB RAM, public

CC Required: No

MongoDB Atlas Free

512MB free MongoDB instance in the cloud. Shared cluster with automated backups and monitoring. No credit card required. Great for learning MongoDB or small projects.

Limits: 512MB storage, shared cluster

CC Required: No

Design Resources

Free design tools and resources for creating graphics, editing photos, prototyping UIs, and sourcing assets. Professional-grade tools available without spending anything.

Canva

Free graphic design platform with templates for social media, presentations, posters, and more. Drag-and-drop interface. Free tier includes millions of stock photos and elements.

Limits: Many templates free, 5GB storage

CC Required: No

Figma

Professional UI/UX design tool in the browser. Free tier: unlimited files, 3 projects, unlimited collaborators. Powerful vector editing, prototyping, and dev handoff. Industry standard.

Limits: 3 projects, unlimited files

CC Required: No

GIMP

GNU Image Manipulation Program. Full Photoshop alternative. Layers, masks, filters, color correction, batch processing. Extensive plugin ecosystem. Free and open-source.

Platforms: Windows, macOS, Linux

CC Required: No

Font Awesome / Iconify

Free icon libraries. Font Awesome offers 2000+ free icons in web font/SVG format. Iconify aggregates 100+ icon sets with 150,000+ icons. Both are open-source and easy to integrate.

Limits: 2000+ free icons

CC Required: No

Unsplash / Pexels

Free stock photography. Millions of high-resolution images free for commercial use. No attribution required (appreciated). Search by keyword, color, or orientation.

Limits: Unlimited downloads

CC Required: No

Learning Platforms

Free platforms to learn programming, design, data science, and more. Quality education without the tuition costs. Many offer certificates of completion and hands-on exercises.

freeCodeCamp

Free interactive coding curriculum. Covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, databases, machine learning. Earn certifications by completing projects. Entirely free, no ads, no paid tiers.

Topics: Full-stack web, Python, ML, more

Site: freecodecamp.org

The Odin Project

Free full-stack web development curriculum. Ruby on Rails and Node.js tracks. Project-based learning with a strong community. Open-source and entirely free.

Topics: Full-stack web development

Site: theodinproject.com

Codecademy Free

Interactive coding exercises in the browser. Free tier includes basic courses on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL. Hands-on learning with instant feedback.

Topics: Web dev, Python, data science

Site: codecademy.com

Google Digital Garage

Free courses on digital marketing, career development, and data analytics. Industry-recognized certificates (including Google Data Analytics Certificate audit option).

Topics: Marketing, data, career

Site: learndigital.withgoogle.com

MIT OpenCourseWare

Free MIT course materials. Full lecture notes, assignments, exams, and video lectures. Computer science, engineering, mathematics, and more. No registration required.

Topics: CS, math, engineering, science

Site: ocw.mit.edu

Khan Academy

Free world-class education for anyone. Interactive exercises and video library covering math, science, computing, history, and test prep. Non-profit and completely free.

Topics: Math, science, computing

Site: khanacademy.org
