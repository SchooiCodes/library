Android Piracy Guide

Modded apps, cracked games, and tools for Android — safety and best sources.

Table of Contents (3 sections)

  • Modded Apps
  • Cracked Games & Emulators
  • App Stores & Tools

Modded Apps

Best sources: Mobilism (forum with modded apps and games — check comments), LiteApks (modded/patched APKs), Sbenny (gaming + apps), HappyMod (modded APK marketplace), APKHome (clean APKs). YouTube mods: ReVanced (no ads, background play, download — use ReVanced Manager, patch official APK), NewPipe (privacy-focused YouTube frontend, no Google account needed). Instagram mods: MyInsta, Instander, AeroInsta.

Cracked Games & Emulators

Game sources: Mobilism Games section, PlatinMods, AndroPalace, 4PDA (Russian forum). Emulators: Yuzu (Switch — runs on powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 2+ devices), AetherSX2 (PS2 development stopped but last version works), Citra (3DS), PPSSPP (PSP — excellent, runs on most devices), Dolphin (GameCube/Wii), DuckStation (PS1). BIOS files: legally required for some emulators, available on archive.org.

App Stores & Tools

F-Droid: FOSS app store, no tracking, auto-updates. Aurora Store: anonymous Google Play client — download apps without Google account, spoof device info. APKMirror: safe APK downloads (verified signatures). Obtainium: auto-update apps from GitHub releases. Lucky Patcher: remove ads, bypass license verification, modify app permissions. Shizuku: grant ADB-level permissions without root — used by Hail (freeze/uninstall system apps).
