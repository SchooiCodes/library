Contrast & Accessibility

WebAIM Contrast Checker

WebAIM's color contrast checker. Enter foreground and background colors to check WCAG compliance. Shows passed/failed ratios for AA and AAA standards.

Contrast Ratio

Simple and fast contrast ratio calculator by Lea Verou. Enter two colors and instantly see the contrast ratio and WCAG grade. Supports HEX, RGB, HSL, and named colors.
