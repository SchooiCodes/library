Software Replacements

Free alternatives to expensive paid software. Replace Photoshop, Office, Premiere, AutoCAD, and more.

All free, all powerful. These open-source and freeware alternatives match or exceed their paid counterparts. Most run on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
Paid Service Free Alternative Type Notes
Adobe Photoshop GIMP / Photopea Free GIMP is the gold standard. Photopea runs in the browser and even opens PSD files.
Microsoft Office LibreOffice / OnlyOffice Free LibreOffice is the most feature-complete. OnlyOffice has better Microsoft compatibility.
Adobe Premiere Pro DaVinci Resolve / Shotcut / Olive Free DaVinci Resolve is professional-grade. Shotcut is beginner-friendly. Olive is the up-and-comer.
AutoCAD FreeCAD / LibreCAD Free FreeCAD for 3D parametric modeling. LibreCAD for 2D CAD drafting.
MATLAB GNU Octave / Scilab Free Octave is almost a drop-in MATLAB replacement. Scilab has its own ecosystem.
Sketch Figma Free Tier Figma is web-based, collaborative, and the industry standard now. Free tier is generous.
Sublime Text VS Code / Notepad++ Free VS Code is the most popular editor. Notepad++ for lightweight Windows editing.
WinRAR 7-Zip Free 7-Zip handles all archive formats, has better compression ratios, and is completely free.
Malwarebytes Windows Defender + AdwCleaner Free Windows Defender is excellent for real-time protection. AdwCleaner for adware cleanup.
CCleaner BleachBit Free BleachBit cleans temporary files, cookies, logs, and shreds free space. Open source too.

Download Managers & Tools

Free tools for downloading media, files, and managing your downloads.

Powerful download manager that handles file-hoster links (Mega, Mediafire, Rapidgator, etc.), captchas automatically, and extracts archives. Essential for premium-free downloading.

Visit

Xtreme Download Manager Free Open Source

IDM alternative with browser integration, video grabbing, and multi-threaded downloads. Works with Chrome, Firefox, and Opera.

Visit

uGet Free Open Source

Lightweight download manager with clipboard monitoring, batch downloads, and integration with aria2. Great alternative to IDM for Linux and Windows.

Visit

yt-dlp Free Open Source

The ultimate command-line downloader for YouTube, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and 1000+ other sites. Download videos, playlists, subtitles, and audio in any format.

GitHub

Seal Free Open Source

Android GUI for yt-dlp. Download videos and audio from YouTube, Twitter, Reddit, and hundreds of other sites directly on your phone. Material Design.

GitHub

SoulSeek Free Open Source

Peer-to-peer file sharing network focused on rare and independent music. Find bootlegs, live recordings, obscure albums, and FLAC files you can't find anywhere else.

Free Workarounds & Bypasses

Tools to bypass paywalls, access blocked content, and browse privately. Use these responsibly and ethically.

Use responsibly. Some of these tools may violate terms of service. They are intended for personal, non-commercial use and educational purposes. Always support content creators when you can.

12ft Ladder Free

Bypass paywalls on news sites, blogs, and articles. Just prepend 12ft.io/ before any URL to view the content without the paywall. Works on many major sites.

Visit

Textise.iitty Free

Strip a web page down to just the text content. Bypasses heavy layouts, blocks ads, and can sometimes get around paywalls. Great for reading-focused browsing.

Visit

Archive.is Free

Webpage archiving and caching tool. Save any page as it appears at a point in time, or view archived versions of pages that are behind paywalls or have been taken down.

Visit

Wikiless Free Open Source

Alternative Wikipedia frontend that doesn't track you. No JavaScript required, no cookies, no data collection. Same content, privacy-friendly wrapper.

Visit

Nitter Free Open Source

Privacy-friendly Twitter/X frontend. View tweets, profiles, and trends without JavaScript, cookies, or tracking. No account needed. RSS feeds available.

Visit

ProxTok Free Open Source

Privacy-friendly TikTok frontend. Watch TikTok videos, view profiles, and search content without tracking or a TikTok account. Self-hostable.

Visit

SearXNG Free Open Source

Privacy-respecting meta-search engine that aggregates results from Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and more. No tracking, no profiling, no ads. Self-host or use a public instance.

Visit

Paywall Bypass Tools Free

Browser extensions and bookmarklets that bypass news paywalls. Tools like Bypass Paywalls Clean, Burlesco, and Anti-Paywall. Works on NYT, Bloomberg, WaPo, and more.

Privacy Tools

Essential free tools to protect your privacy and security online.

Signal Free Open Source

The gold standard for encrypted messaging. End-to-end encrypted texts, voice/video calls, and file sharing. No ads, no tracking, no data collection.

Visit

Proton Mail Free Tier

End-to-end encrypted email service based in Switzerland. Free tier includes 500MB storage, up to 150 messages/day, and full encryption. No phone number required.

Visit

Bitwarden Free Open Source

Best free password manager. Unlimited passwords, unlimited devices, passkey support, and secure sharing. Encrypted cloud sync across all your devices.

Visit

VeraCrypt Free Open Source

Full-disk and container encryption. Create encrypted volumes, encrypt entire drives, or hide encrypted data within other data. Successor to TrueCrypt.

Visit

Tor Browser Free Open Source

Anonymous browsing via the Tor network. Routes your traffic through multiple relays to hide your location and identity. Essential for privacy and bypassing censorship.

Visit

uBlock Origin Free Open Source

The best ad blocker and content filter. Lightweight, efficient, and blocks ads, trackers, malware domains, and annoyances. Essential for every browser.

Visit

Firefox + arkenfox Free Open Source

Firefox hardened with arkenfox user.js — a comprehensive set of privacy-enhancing configurations. Disables telemetry, fingerprinting, WebRTC leaks, and tracking.
