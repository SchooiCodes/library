Free weather API with current conditions, forecasts, historical data, and weather maps. Supports city name, zip code, and GPS coordinates. Free tier allows 60 calls/min.

Access Spotify's catalog — tracks, albums, artists, playlists, and user data. Free tier with OAuth authentication. Great for music discovery apps and playlists.
