🖼 Free Stock Resources

High-quality free stock photos, videos, audio, and icons for your projects — all completely free to use.

Stock Photos

Unsplash

The internet's source of freely usable images. Millions of high-resolution photos contributed by photographers worldwide. Free for commercial and non-commercial use.

Visit

Pexels

Free stock photos and videos shared by talented creators. High-quality, curated collection with an easy-to-use search engine and API access.

Visit

Pixabay

Massive library of free stock photos, vectors, illustrations, and videos. Over 2 million assets all released under the Pixabay license — free for any use.

Visit

Burst (Shopify)

Free stock photos for e-commerce and websites. Curated collections by Shopify with a focus on business, lifestyle, and product photography.

Visit

Picsum

Lorem Ipsum for photos. Generate placeholder images with customizable dimensions, random seeds, and caching. Perfect for prototyping and mockups.

Visit

Stock Videos

Pexels Videos

Free stock video footage contributed by the community. High-quality clips in various categories. Free for commercial use with no attribution required.

Visit

Coverr

Beautiful, free stock videos for your websites and projects. Curated collection updated weekly. Use for backgrounds, headers, and promotional content.

Visit

Mixkit

Free stock videos, motion graphics, templates, and sound effects. High-quality assets for video creators, with a mix of professional and casual footage.

Visit

Music & Audio

Freesound

Collaborative database of audio snippets, samples, recordings, and bleeps. Creative Commons licensed sounds for music production and sound design.

Visit

Pixabay Music

Royalty-free music and sound effects from Pixabay's community of musicians. Download and use in your projects without attribution.

Visit

Uppbeat

Free music for creators, including YouTubers and podcasters. Curated tracks from independent artists with clear licensing for online content.

Visit

Bensound

Royalty-free music for videos, podcasts, and presentations. Wide range of genres from cinematic to corporate. Free with attribution or paid license for royalty-free.

Visit

Icons

Font Awesome

The world's most popular icon library. Thousands of free icons in solid, regular, and brands styles. Icon font or SVG framework. Pro tier available with more icons.

Visit

Heroicons

Beautiful hand-crafted SVG icons by the makers of Tailwind CSS. Available in outline and solid styles. Easy to use in web projects with copy-paste SVG.

Visit

Lucide

Open-source icon library based on Feather Icons. Clean, consistent, and customizable SVG icons. Available as an npm package for React, Vue, and Svelte.

Visit

Tabler Icons

Over 4,000 free open-source SVG icons. Consistent stroke-based design, available in multiple sizes. Works well with React, Vue, Angular, and plain HTML.

Visit
