Features

  • Real-Debrid: Best for Stremio + Torrentio. Cheapest. Unlimited torrent storage. 80+ hosters. Fast API.
  • AllDebrid: Similar to Real-Debrid. Slightly fewer hosters. Multiple concurrent subscriptions allowed.
  • Premiumize: Most expensive but most features. Includes VPN (1 Gbps). CDN for fast worldwide access. Usenet support. Swiss-based, no logs.

Speed Tests

General speed observations: All three services can saturate a typical home internet connection (500 Mbps - 1 Gbps). Real-Debrid and AllDebrid have servers in France. Premiumize has a global CDN for faster worldwide access. Real-world differences are minimal for most users.

Which to Choose

  • Choose Real-Debrid if: You want the cheapest option and primarily use Stremio + Torrentio.
  • Choose AllDebrid if: Real-Debrid is down or you want multiple concurrent subscriptions.
  • Choose Premiumize if: You want an all-in-one solution with VPN, Usenet, and cloud storage, and don’t mind paying more.

Cached Torrent Availability

One of the most important factors when choosing a debrid service is the size and speed of their torrent cache:

  • Real-Debrid: The largest cache by far. Most popular movies and TV shows (within the last 5 years) are cached. New releases are cached within hours of hitting public trackers. The cache size is estimated at 50+ PB.
  • AllDebrid: Smaller cache but growing. Less likely to have niche or older content cached. Better for mainstream Hollywood content.
  • Premiumize: Smaller cache but has a unique "CDN" feature that streams cached content from servers close to you. Good for rare content that other users have cached.
  • Cache check: Torrentio shows [RD+] for cached, [RD] for uncached. In Kodi, Seren/Fen indicate cache status. You can also check manually by adding a magnet to the service’s web interface and seeing if it starts instantly (cached) or needs to download (uncached).

How Debrid Works Technically

Understanding the technical architecture of debrid services helps you troubleshoot and choose wisely:

  • File hoster unrestriction: When you submit a hoster link, the debrid service downloads the file from the hoster onto their own servers (using their premium account). They then give you a direct HTTPS link. Your IP never contacts the file hoster directly.
  • Torrent handling: You submit a magnet/.torrent. The debrid service acts as a BitTorrent client on their end, downloading the torrent to their servers. Once complete, the files are available as direct HTTPS downloads. The service may also cache the torrent for other users.
  • Streaming: For real-time streaming (Stremio, Kodi), the debrid service generates a direct HTTPS stream URL. The media player fetches chunks on the fly. The service handles range requests (HTTP partial content) for seek support.
  • CDN delivery: Premiumize uses a global CDN (Content Delivery Network) with edge servers worldwide. Real-Debrid and AllDebrid serve primarily from their European data centers.

Use Cases

  • Stremio + Torrentio: Real-Debrid is the king. Largest cache, cheapest price, perfect integration. Torrentio was designed around Real-Debrid’s API.
  • Kodi + Seren/Fen: Real-Debrid or Premiumize both work well. Real-Debrid has better caching for Kodi addons. Premiumize offers VPN integration for users who want an all-in-one.
  • JDownloader (bulk downloading): Real-Debrid has the most supported hosters for DDL sites. AllDebrid is also good. Premiumize’s 500-1000 GB storage cap can be a limitation for heavy downloading.
  • Browser downloads (direct links): All three work. Real-Debrid and AllDebrid generate direct download links. Premiumize’s CDN provides faster downloads for non-EU users.
  • Usenet replacement: Premiumize is the only debrid service with Usenet support. If you want a debrid service that can also handle Usenet, Premiumize is your only option.

Account Security

  • API tokens: Never share your API token. It grants full access to your account. Regenerate immediately if compromised.
  • Password best practices: Use unique, complex passwords stored in a password manager. Change periodically.
  • Two-factor authentication: None of the major debrid services support 2FA. This makes strong passwords doubly important.
  • IP restrictions: Real-Debrid and Premiumize restrict to 1 concurrent IP. AllDebrid allows 3. If someone steals your credentials, they can use your account from a different IP — but you’ll notice when you get IP conflict errors.
  • Session management: Check active sessions in your account dashboard. Log out all sessions if you see unfamiliar activity.
