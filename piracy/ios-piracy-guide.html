iOS Piracy Guide — Sideloading & App Stores

Master iOS piracy: AltStore, SideStore, TrollStore, signing services, AppCake, and emulators on iPhone and iPad.

Table of Contents

AltStore

AltStore is a free sideloading tool for iOS that lets you install apps outside the App Store using your Apple ID. Apps last 7 days before needing refresh. Free Apple IDs can sideload only 3 apps.

  • AltServer: Desktop component that handles app signing.
  • Best for: Emulators (Delta), modified apps (Spotify++, YouTube++).

SideStore

SideStore is an AltStore alternative that doesn’t require a computer to refresh apps. Uses WireGuard VPN to refresh apps over the air. True untethered sideloading.

TrollStore

TrollStore is a permanent sideloading tool for iOS versions up to 16.6.1. Apps never expire, no refresh needed, no app limit. Exploits a CoreTrust bug. Does not work on iOS 17+.

AppCake

AppCake is a third-party app store with pre-cracked apps. Install via enterprise certificate (may get revoked) or sideload via AltStore. Alternatives: TutuApp, Panda Helper, Ignition.

Signing Services

Paid signing services (Signulous, AppDB, iPAStore) provide enterprise certificates for $10-20/year. Apps last up to 1 year. No computer needed after initial setup.

Emulators

  • Delta (AltStore) — NES, SNES, N64, GBA, DS emulator. Beautiful interface.
  • PPSSPP — PSP emulator. Runs on iOS via AltStore/TrollStore.
  • DolphiniOS — GameCube and Wii emulator (JIT required).
  • RetroArch — Multi-system emulator. Covers Atari to PS1.
  • Provenance — Multi-system emulator with a modern UI. Supports NES, SNES, Genesis, Game Boy, N64, PSP, and PlayStation. Available via AltStore and TrollStore.
  • Play! — Sony PlayStation 2 emulator for iOS. Early stage but playable for many titles.

AltStore Setup Guide

Setting up AltStore requires a computer for the initial installation. Follow these steps:

  1. Download AltServer: Download from altstore.io for macOS or Windows. Install it on your computer.
  2. Connect your device: Plug your iPhone or iPad into your computer via USB. Trust the computer if prompted.
  3. Install AltStore: On macOS, click the AltServer icon in the menu bar, select "Install AltStore" and choose your device. On Windows, right-click the AltServer system tray icon and select "Install AltStore."
  4. Enter Apple ID: You will be prompted for your Apple ID and app-specific password. Apple IDs are free to create. The credentials are sent only to Apple for signing.
  5. Trust the profile: On your iOS device, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → trust your Apple ID profile.
  6. Install apps: Open AltStore on your device. Browse the "Browse" tab for available apps, or sideload your own .ipa files via the "+" button.

Apps signed with a free Apple ID expire after 7 days. Reconnect to AltServer and refresh them before expiry. Auto-refresh requires AltServer running on your local network.

TrollStore Usage

TrollStore is the holy grail of iOS sideloading. It exploits the CoreTrust bug on iOS 14.0 – 16.6.1 to install apps that never expire. Installation methods vary by iOS version:

  • iOS 14.0 – 15.4.1: Use the GTA Car Tracker exploit (install via AltStore, then install TrollStore through the app).
  • iOS 15.5 – 16.6.1: Use Misaka or Puaf exploits. Check the TrollStore GitHub for the latest installation guide.
  • What you can do with TrollStore: Install any .ipa permanently, no 3-app limit, no 7-day expiry, full system access for apps. Use Filza for file system browsing, iTorrent for torrenting, and uYouPlus for YouTube without ads.
Warning: TrollStore does not work on iOS 17 or later. Apple patched the CoreTrust bug in iOS 17. If you are on iOS 17, your options are limited to AltStore/SideStore and signing services.

Jailbreak Options

Jailbreaking removes Apple’s restrictions entirely, giving you root access and the ability to install tweaks, themes, and system modifications. Modern jailbreaks are semi-untethered (re-apply after reboot):

Jailbreak iOS Support Device
Dopamine15.0 – 16.6.1 (arm64e)iPhone XS and newer
Palera1n15.0 – 17.x (arm64)iPhone X and older (A11)
XinaA1515.0 – 15.4.1iPhone 6S – 13

Dopamine is the most popular modern jailbreak for A12+ devices. It is rootless (safer), has full tweak injection, and supports iOS 15 – 16.6.1. Install via TrollStore or AltStore. Palera1n works on older devices (A8-A11) up to iOS 17 using the checkm8 bootrom exploit, which cannot be patched by Apple.

Modded Apps (uYouPlus, Spotilife)

Modified versions of popular apps provide premium features for free:

  • uYouPlus / uYouEnhanced: YouTube without ads, background playback, video downloading, sponsor block. Download .ipa from GitHub and sideload via AltStore/TrollStore.
  • Spotilife / SpotC++: Spotify Premium features unlocked: unlimited skips, on-demand playback, no ads, high-quality audio. Sideload the .ipa or use jailbreak tweak.
  • Instagram++ (Rocket for Instagram): Download photos/videos, view full-size, see who viewed your profile, disable read receipts.
  • Facebook++: Download videos, ad-free browsing, message customization.
  • Twitter++ (Tweaked Twitter): Ad-free timeline, video downloads, tweet filtering, undo tweets.

Find modded .ipa files on sites like CyPwn, AppDB, iOSGods, and GitHub. Always scan .ipa files before installing and prefer open-source mods.

iTorrent Usage

iTorrent is a full-featured torrent client for iOS. It requires TrollStore or a jailbreak (file system access for saving torrents):

  • Install the iTorrent .ipa via TrollStore.
  • Open the app and grant file access to your preferred download directory.
  • Add torrents via magnet links, .torrent files, or search within the app.
  • Configure upload/download speed limits, sequential downloading (for streaming), and DHT/PEX/LSD settings.
  • Use with a VPN on the device for privacy, or use a seedbox and FTP your media files.

Alternative: qBittorrent Remote is not a torrent client itself, but a remote control for qBittorrent running on your desktop or seedbox.

Music Downloading on iOS

  • Deemix: Download FLAC/MP3 from Deezer. Requires sideloading. Server-side component handles the actual downloading.
  • FreeBird / DownWell: iOS music downloaders that integrate with Streaming Services.
  • iTunes File Sharing: Drag and drop MP3/FLAC files to your iPhone via Finder (macOS) or iTunes (Windows).
  • Documents by Readdle: Full file manager with browser. Download music from DDL sites directly to your iPhone.
  • VLC for iOS: Plays almost any audio format. Transfer music via Wi-Fi Upload, iTunes sharing, or cloud services.

Ebook Management on iOS

  • Marvin Ebook Reader: Supports EPUB, PDF, CBR/CBZ. Connects to Calibre and cloud services.
  • KyBook 3: Universal ebook reader with OPDS support, Calibre integration, cloud sync, text-to-speech.
  • Calibre Companion: Control your Calibre library wirelessly. Import and organize ebooks.
  • Yomi (Manga reader): Browse and read manga from various online sources. Supports multiple providers.
  • PB Heartbeat (Paperback): Manga reader similar to Tachiyomi, with source integration.

VPNs & DNS Profiles for iOS

iOS imposes limits on VPN routing — not all traffic can be forced through the VPN without a jailbreak. Here are the best approaches:

  • VPN apps with on-demand: Mullvad, ProtonVPN, and Windscribe have excellent iOS apps with kill switch and on-demand connection.
  • DNS profiles for ad blocking: Install a DNS profile like AdGuard DNS or NextDNS that blocks trackers and ads system-wide. Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → DNS → add configuration.
  • Mullvad on iOS: Supports WireGuard, ad blocking via DNS, always-on VPN. No port forwarding on iOS, but the encrypted tunnel protects all traffic.
  • Split tunneling on iOS: Not natively supported without a jailbreak. If you need split tunnel, jailbreak and use a tweak like VPNSystem.
Tip: Use AdGuard Pro (paid) or NextDNS (free tier) for system-wide ad blocking without a VPN. This blocks ads in Safari, apps, and games, and protects your privacy across all connections.
