iOS Piracy Guide — Sideloading & App Stores

Master iOS piracy: AltStore, SideStore, TrollStore, signing services, AppCake, and emulators on iPhone and iPad.

Table of Contents

AltStore

AltStore is a free sideloading tool for iOS that lets you install apps outside the App Store using your Apple ID. Apps last 7 days before needing refresh. Free Apple IDs can sideload only 3 apps.

  • AltServer: Desktop component that handles app signing.
  • Best for: Emulators (Delta), modified apps (Spotify++, YouTube++).

SideStore

SideStore is an AltStore alternative that doesn’t require a computer to refresh apps. Uses WireGuard VPN to refresh apps over the air. True untethered sideloading.

TrollStore

TrollStore is a permanent sideloading tool for iOS versions up to 16.6.1. Apps never expire, no refresh needed, no app limit. Exploits a CoreTrust bug. Does not work on iOS 17+.

AppCake

AppCake is a third-party app store with pre-cracked apps. Install via enterprise certificate (may get revoked) or sideload via AltStore. Alternatives: TutuApp, Panda Helper, Ignition.

Signing Services

Paid signing services (Signulous, AppDB, iPAStore) provide enterprise certificates for $10-20/year. Apps last up to 1 year. No computer needed after initial setup.

Emulators

  • Delta (AltStore) — NES, SNES, N64, GBA, DS emulator. Beautiful interface.
  • PPSSPP — PSP emulator. Runs on iOS via AltStore/TrollStore.
  • DolphiniOS — GameCube and Wii emulator (JIT required).
  • RetroArch — Multi-system emulator. Covers Atari to PS1.
