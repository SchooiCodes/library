Disclaimer & Safety

This page is for educational purposes only. Accessing copyrighted material without permission may be illegal in your country. Always use a reputable VPN, avoid downloading executables from untrusted sources, and scan everything with antivirus software. You are responsible for your own actions.

Safety Guide

Before you start, understand the risks and how to protect yourself.

Use a VPN

A VPN encrypts your traffic and hides your IP address. Essential for torrenting and visiting pirate sites. Mullvad, ProtonVPN, and AirVPN are trusted options that don't log.

Bind VPN to Client

In qBittorrent, bind your VPN interface under Settings > Advanced > Network Interface. This stops all traffic if the VPN drops — preventing IP leaks.

VirusTotal Everything

Before running any downloaded file, upload it to VirusTotal.com to check against 70+ antivirus engines. Avoid files with few or no detections from unknown uploaders.

Private Mode

Use a separate browser profile or container for piracy sites. Block scripts with uBlock Origin, use DNS-over-HTTPS, and clear cookies regularly.

Media Servers & Self-Hosting

Run your own media server to organize and stream your downloaded content anywhere.

Plex

The most popular media server. Organize movies, TV, music, and photos. Stream to any device. Pair with Sonarr/Radarr for automation. Plexamp for music.

Plex

Jellyfin

Fully open-source Plex alternative. No paid subscriptions, no telemetry. Supports hardware transcoding, plugins, and all major clients.

Jellyfin

Automation Stack

Sonarr (TV) + Radarr (Movies) + Lidarr (Music) + Readarr (Books) + Bazarr (Subtitles) + Prowlarr (Indexers). Set it and forget it.

Cloud & Remote Access

Expose your media server with a reverse proxy (Nginx, Caddy, Cloudflare Tunnel). Use Tailscale or WireGuard for secure remote access without port forwarding.
