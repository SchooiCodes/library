Digital Estate Planning

Plan for your digital afterlife - password inheritance via Bitwarden, dead man switches, document storage, and family instructions.

What is a Digital Will?

A digital will ensures your passwords, accounts, files, cryptocurrency, and online services are accessible to loved ones after you pass away.

Without one: Family cannot access email, crypto is lost forever, cloud storage is locked, subscriptions bill forever.

Components: Password inheritance, asset inventory, instructions document, dead man switch, physical documents.

Password Inheritance (Bitwarden)

Bitwarden Emergency Access is the most practical solution.

# Settings - Emergency Access - Add Contact
# Set 7-day waiting period
# Contact requests access, no response = access granted

# Offline: Write master password on paper, seal in safe

Dead Man Switch

Options: Dead Man Switch (.org), Google Inactive Account Manager, self-hosted cron script, physical envelope with trusted person.

Important Documents

Store in Bitwarden: Will location, life insurance, property deeds, investment accounts, crypto seed phrases, safe combos, bank accounts, insurance policies.

Crypto: Never store seed phrases digitally. Use paper/metal backup. Consider Shamir Secret Sharing.

Instructions for Family

Create step-by-step instructions for your family:

  1. Contact executor and lawyer, retrieve envelope
  2. Handle email, bank, mortgage, insurance (48h)
  3. Social media, cloud storage, domains, crypto (1 week)
  4. Cancel or transfer subscriptions (2 weeks)

Record a video: 5-minute explanation of your plan.
